Ringtone 4: A Wind Named W

This ring-tone contains gravitational wave signals from an inspiral system, consisting of blackholes of 0.5 solar masses and 0.7 solar masses, at an inclination angle of 45 degrees. The signal lasts for 2s, beginning at 1240Hz, and ending at 2088Hz. In making this ring-tone, only a section of this, the first 1.2 seconds, is used. A time-reversed version of this section is joined to the end of the original section to produce the 'chromatic-scale' at the beginning of the ring-tone. The last part section of the ring-tone is made from overlapping the time-reversed version and the original version of the section of the signal used. The whole ring-tone sounds like a siren.
