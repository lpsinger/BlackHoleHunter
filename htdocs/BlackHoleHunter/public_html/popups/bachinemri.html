Ringtone 1: Bach in EMRI

This ringtone is made with gravitational wave sounds produced by extreme mass ratio inspirals (EMRIs), that is when a black hole with mass comparable to the sun inspirals into a supermassive black hole, like that believed to exist at the centre of the galaxy. We took the waveform and low pass filtered it to make a smooth sound and then used them to emulate J. S. Bach's "Preludium and Fugue in A minor" producing the "Bach In EMRI" ringtone.
