Ringtone 3: Bachanory

This ringtone used samples from the waveform of a (5,10) solar mass system inclined at an angle of 0 degrees to the line of sight. The melody was created by taking a sample roughly corresponding to an E-note, and pitch-shifting to create the other notes of the melody. To alleviate any artefacts due to the sharp start and end of the sample, short fade-in and fade-outs were applied. The tune is a slow version of a section of Toccata and Fugue by J.S. Bach, but transposed from Dminor to Aminor.
